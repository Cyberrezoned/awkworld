# **App Name**: Awkward: Elegance in Motion

## Core Features:

- 3D Product Showcase: Display products in an immersive 3D environment with reflective materials and dynamic lighting.
- Animated Scrolling: Implement multi-stage animated scroll paths with motion blur and depth of field to create a cinematic experience.
- Interactive 3D Navigation: Use a floating nav orb that expands into a 3D radial menu with scale, glow, and rotation effects.
- AI Stylist: Use the 'Awkward Assistant' to suggest fits based on body type, weather, and trend data; the assistant uses AI as a tool to incorporate external information into its suggestions.
- AI Visual Generation: Leverage Genkit to generate animated mockups and dynamic 3D product renders from product data.
- Dynamic Dark Mode: Implement scroll-reactive lighting simulation to transition through morning, dusk, and night tones.
- MongoDB Integration: Integrate MongoDB to save product information, user details, and AI-generated content.
- Animation features: Add animation features like gsap or something similar

## Style Guidelines:

- Background: Deep black (#0B0B0B) for a cinematic feel.
- Accent: Golden highlight (#E5B80B) for luxurious touch.
- Glow: Electric violet (#7D3EFF) for a futuristic neon effect. The color palette combines analogous colors on the warm (gold) and cool (violet) sides of the spectrum.
- Headlines: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, to provide a techy feel.
- Body: 'Inter', sans-serif, for a modern look.
- Minimalist 3D icons with a subtle luminescent glow on hover.
- Subtle inertia-based motion for smooth, natural transitions; avoid sharp cuts.
